What Are the Benin Bronzes?

The Benin Bronzes are beautiful pieces of art made from bronze, brass, and ivory. They tell stories of kings, warriors, animals, and everyday life in the old Benin Kingdom.

How Are the Benin Bronzes Made?

  • Step 1: Make a clay model.
  • Step 2: Cover it with wax to form the shape.
  • Step 3: Cover the wax with clay, leaving a hole.
  • Step 4: Melt out the wax with heat.
  • Step 5: Pour in molten bronze.
  • Step 6: Break off the clay to reveal the sculpture.
